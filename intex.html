Technical Events

Tech Exegesis

This is an electronics and communication-based open topic forum where the forum is open for the Brilliant ideas to flow over with your priceless scripts. A 4-person maximum individual group with a 5- to 7-minute presentation period.

Smarticus

On the day of the event, we'll present a few hypothetical situations involving current social problems. You will proceed with your company concepts in order to provide remedies for the situation.

Techno War

General discussion about something expressing different opinions.
Topic will be given on spot


Trial by Trivia

A technical quiz tournament called Trial by Trivia features questions with a technical focus. It has both general aptitude questions and questions specific to the ECE Department. It has a one-hour time limit and is a multiple-choice question.

Non-Technical Events

Clash of Techies

Knockout Tournament will be conducted.Cash Rewards will be provided to winners.

Treasure Hunt

Timed games called "treasure hunts" require players to find concealed items by following a trail of hints.

Connections

In Connect It, match the pairs of pictures to make all the lights go green! It's up to you to figure out the link between pictures.

Nova Crafty

Freehand painting is the term used to describe patterns, pictures, and textures made solely with paint on flat surfaces. (As opposed to when we are utilising paint to highlight the sculpted details that are right into the surface.)
